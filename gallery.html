Our Eco Journey

Sandalu Eco Resort was established in 2010 with a vision to create a sanctuary where luxury meets sustainability. Our founders, passionate conservationists, transformed 50 acres of wilderness into an eco-paradise while preserving 90% of the natural habitat.

We've pioneered sustainable tourism practices in Sri Lanka, from our solar-powered facilities to our zero-waste initiatives. Our resort has won multiple awards for environmental stewardship while providing unforgettable experiences for our guests.

Today, we continue to innovate, working with local communities and conservation organizations to protect Yala's unique ecosystem for future generations.
