Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about visiting our resort

What is the best way to reach Sandalu Eco Resort?

The most convenient way to reach us is by car. We're approximately a 5-hour drive from Colombo. Alternatively, you can fly to Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport (HRI), which is about 1.5 hours away. We can arrange airport transfers for our guests with advance notice.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your reservation free of charge up to 14 days before your scheduled arrival. Cancellations made 7-14 days prior to arrival will incur a 50% charge. Cancellations within 7 days of arrival will be charged the full amount. We recommend travel insurance for unforeseen circumstances.

Do you offer safari packages?

Yes, we offer several safari packages that include accommodation, meals, and guided tours through Yala National Park. Our experienced naturalist guides will help you spot leopards, elephants, and hundreds of bird species. Packages can be customized to suit your preferences.

Is the resort suitable for children?

Absolutely! We welcome families and have special activities designed for children, including nature walks, wildlife education sessions, and supervised craft activities. We also offer family suites and babysitting services upon request.

What sustainability practices do you follow?

As an eco-resort, we're committed to sustainable tourism. Our practices include solar power generation, rainwater harvesting, organic waste composting, plastic-free operations, and sourcing local organic produce. We also contribute to wildlife conservation efforts in the Yala region.
