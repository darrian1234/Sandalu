Welcome to Sandalu Eco Resort

Nestled in the heart of Yala, Sri Lanka, Sandalu Eco Resort is a haven for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful retreat. Our resort is designed to blend seamlessly with the surrounding environment, offering you an authentic experience without compromising on comfort.

We are committed to sustainable tourism practices, from our solar-powered facilities to our zero-waste policy. Our mission is to preserve the natural beauty of Yala for generations to come while providing our guests with an unforgettable experience.

Our team of local experts is dedicated to sharing the rich cultural heritage and biodiversity of the region, ensuring your stay is both educational and rejuvenating.
